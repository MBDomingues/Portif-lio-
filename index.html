My Projects

The goal of the AdCoin app is to provide a simple, secure, and effective platform to help users manage their finances, even without prior knowledge of financial matters. The proposal aims to serve individuals dissatisfied with overly complex solutions available in the market, offering intuitive tools to manage salary, expenses, debts, and goals. The app creates a personalized roadmap to guide users toward achieving their financial objectives in a practical and accessible way. Currently, the project is in progress, being developed with a focus on user experience and delivering effective results, with the support and encouragement of my university, FIAP..

Access Project

This project is being developed in partnership between the company LetsSign and my university FIAP. The proposal involves solving a problem related to the contestation of electronic signatures. The goal is to create a solution that allows for more reliable electronic document signing, ensuring authenticity and reducing the possibility of disputes. The aim is to make electronic documents safer, more truthful, and incontestable.
